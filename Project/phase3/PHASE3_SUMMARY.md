### Q: "The standard RL algorithm is Deep Q Learning, right?"

**A:** Yes! DQN is perfect for this problem because:
- **Discrete actions** (4 choices)
- **Continuous states** (prices, rates can be any value)
- **Off-policy** (can learn from random exploration)
- **Well-studied** (lots of resources, works reliably)

Other options (but DQN is best here):
- PPO (policy gradient) - overkill for discrete actions
- A3C - more complex, harder to debug
- SAC - designed for continuous actions
